Helena Roy

I am a PhD economist at Stanford University. My fields are behavioral economics and industrial organisation. I'm interested in how people access and process information, particularly with respect to healthcare decisions. Current projects include work on the use of patient satisfaction scores, physician experience and the maternal health gap in the US, and education and the digital divide in internet access.
